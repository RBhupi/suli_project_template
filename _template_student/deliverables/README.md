# Deliverables

Upload your final outputs here before the end of your internship. Any format is fine — pdf, pptx, tex, docx, md, zip.

## Expected deliverables

### SULI Students
- **Poster** — presented at the Argonne symposium (pdf or pptx)
- **Research report** — written paper describing your project and findings
- **Abstract for general audience** — one page, non-technical summary
- **One-page peer review** — review of another intern's abstract

### Other Interns
- **Oral presentation** slides
- **Abstract for general audience**
- **White paper or publication** — 4–8 pages (LaTeX recommended; no Word)
- **Blog-style science article** (if applicable)

Upload files directly into this folder. Name them clearly: `poster_alice_smith.pdf`, `report_alice_smith.pdf`, etc.
